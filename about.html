Our Story

Sankalp Siksha was founded with a vision to provide high-quality education to students in Muzaffarpur and surrounding areas.

Under the leadership of our Director, Neha Singh, Sankalp Siksha has grown into a trusted coaching institute known for conceptual clarity, disciplined learning, and consistent results. We focus on building strong fundamentals, problem-solving skills, and confidence in every student.
